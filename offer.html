Our Offer

We have the necessary knowledge to conduct a mandatory energy audit of a company, in accordance with the requirements of PN-EN 16247. It is worth remembering that an audit is not only an obligation, but most of all a chance to generate additional savings. We handle all formalities on behalf of the client. After performing the necessary analyzes and studies, we report their effects to the ERO.

One of the most important elements of our company's mission is to optimize energy management in the industry, therefore our audit must bring real savings.

Energy clusters have been one of the most developing trends in the energy industry in recent years. In recent years, more than 100 such organizations have been established all over Poland, and their structures include the most significant companies from the industrial, mining and energy sectors.

It is no secret that SCI is an undisputed leader in supporting the development of energy clusters. Thanks to our support, a dozen or so organizations of this type have been created. Our knowledge in this area was also used by the Ministry of Energy, commissioned by us to carry out a multidisciplinary expertise entitled "The concept of the functioning of energy clusters in Poland". This document is a starting point for detailed consideration in the design opdpowiednich law.
