About us

Among last years we realized many multidisplinary expertises, analyzes and audits in field of distributed energy resources energetic efficiency.

Our mission is transformation of widely understood energy sector in Poland. By our knowledge and experience we try to support changes tending to responsible and effective management of energy. We believe that our actions will help to increase share of modern and enviroment friendly technologies for energy manufacturing

We help inverstors to start producing clean energy, and energy consumers in the rational use of heat and electricity. Our opinions and articles have appeared many times in the pages the most important industry magazines and portals. We perform regularly as experts in the field of energy at prestigious economic conferences.

If you want to become part of the ongoing energy revolution and want to reduce energy consumption or switch to zero-emission generation technologies, please contact us and learn how we can help you
